Our Story

Honest HVAC.
No Surprises.

We started CoolBreeze in 2008 because we were tired of seeing homeowners get overcharged and oversold by big HVAC companies. We're a family business — and we treat your home like it's ours.

What started as a two-person operation out of a single truck has grown into one of Lee County's most trusted HVAC companies. But we've never lost sight of what got us here: showing up on time, doing honest work, and charging a fair price.

Every technician on our team is Florida-licensed, background-checked, and trained to treat your home with respect. We don't do high-pressure upsells. If your system can be repaired, we'll repair it. Period.
